Introduction Pendahuluan

It's Not About the Shoes Ini Bukan Tentang Sepatu

"Zappos is a customer service company that just happens to sell shoes." "Zappos adalah perusahaan layanan pelanggan yang kebetulan menjual sepatu."

Tony Hsieh (pronounced Shay) built Zappos from a struggling startup into a billion-dollar giant acquired by Amazon. He did this by violating almost every standard corporate rule. This book is his story of how prioritizing Culture and Happiness (for employees and customers) led to massive financial success.

Tony Hsieh (dibaca Shay) membangun Zappos dari startup yang kesulitan menjadi raksasa bernilai miliaran dolar yang diakuisisi Amazon. Dia melakukannya dengan melanggar hampir setiap aturan standar perusahaan. Buku ini adalah kisahnya tentang bagaimana memprioritaskan Budaya dan Kebahagiaan (bagi karyawan dan pelanggan) menghasilkan kesuksesan finansial yang masif.

The Core Philosophy

Filosofi Inti

  • 1. Customer Service as Marketing: Instead of spending money on ads, invest it in customer service. Let your customers do the marketing for you (Word of Mouth). Layanan Pelanggan sebagai Pemasaran: Daripada buang uang untuk iklan, investasikan di layanan pelanggan. Biarkan pelanggan melakukan pemasaran untuk Anda (Dari Mulut ke Mulut).
  • 2. Culture is Brand: Your brand is just a lagging indicator of your culture. If the culture is toxic, the brand will eventually fail. Budaya adalah Merek: Merek Anda hanyalah indikator tertinggal dari budaya Anda. Jika budayanya beracun, mereknya akhirnya akan gagal.
  • 3. Chase Vision, Not Money: Money follows passion and purpose, not the other way around. Kejar Visi, Bukan Uang: Uang mengikuti gairah dan tujuan, bukan sebaliknya.
Part 1

Profits (The Early Mistakes) Profit (Kesalahan Awal)

Before Zappos, Tony founded LinkExchange. He sold it to Microsoft for $265 million when he was just 24. It sounds like a dream, but Tony was miserable.

Sebelum Zappos, Tony mendirikan LinkExchange. Dia menjualnya ke Microsoft seharga $265 juta saat usianya baru 24 tahun. Terdengar seperti mimpi, tapi Tony merasa sengsara.

Why He Quit LinkExchange

Mengapa Dia Keluar dari LinkExchange

Initially, he hired friends who fit the culture. As they grew fast, they hired anyone with a pulse and skills.

The Result: The culture died. Office politics took over. Tony dreaded going to his own company. He realized that Company Culture was more important than pure growth or money. He walked away from millions in earn-out bonuses just to escape the toxic environment he created.

Awalnya, dia merekrut teman yang cocok dengan budaya. Karena tumbuh cepat, mereka merekrut siapa saja yang punya keahlian.

Hasilnya: Budaya mati. Politik kantor mengambil alih. Tony takut pergi ke perusahaannya sendiri. Dia sadar bahwa Budaya Perusahaan lebih penting daripada pertumbuhan murni atau uang. Dia meninggalkan bonus jutaan dolar hanya untuk kabur dari lingkungan beracun yang dia ciptakan.

The Lesson of Poker Pelajaran dari Poker

Tony was a serious poker player. He applied poker lessons to business:
1. Table Selection: It doesn't matter how good you are if you sit at a table with people much better than you. In business, choose an industry (table) where you can win.
2. Long Term Thinking: Do not tilt (get emotional) over short-term losses. Focus on the Expected Value (EV) of decisions over 1,000 hands.

Tony adalah pemain poker serius. Dia menerapkan pelajaran poker ke bisnis:
1. Pemilihan Meja: Tidak peduli seberapa jago Anda jika duduk di meja dengan orang yang jauh lebih jago. Dalam bisnis, pilih industri (meja) di mana Anda bisa menang.
2. Berpikir Jangka Panjang: Jangan emosi (tilt) karena kerugian jangka pendek. Fokus pada Nilai Harapan (EV) dari keputusan lebih dari 1.000 putaran.

Part 2

Passion: Building the Culture Gairah: Membangun Budaya

At Zappos, culture is not a plaque on the wall. It is the hiring and firing criteria. If you are a genius but destroy the culture, you are fired.

Di Zappos, budaya bukan plakat di dinding. Itu adalah kriteria perekrutan dan pemecatan. Jika Anda jenius tapi merusak budaya, Anda dipecat.

Case Study: "The Offer" (Pay to Quit)

Studi Kasus: "Tawaran" (Dibayar untuk Keluar)

Zappos has a 4-week rigorous training program. At the end of the training, they make a shocking offer to every new hire:

Zappos memiliki program pelatihan ketat 4 minggu. Di akhir pelatihan, mereka memberikan tawaran mengejutkan kepada setiap karyawan baru:

"We will pay you for your time worked, PLUS a $2,000 bonus if you quit right now." "Kami akan membayar waktu kerja Anda, DITAMBAH bonus $2.000 jika Anda keluar sekarang juga."

Why? Tony wants people who are there for the culture and the long-term vision, not just a paycheck. If someone is willing to take $2,000 to leave, they don't have the commitment Zappos needs. Less than 1% take the offer.

Mengapa? Tony menginginkan orang yang ada di sana demi budaya dan visi jangka panjang, bukan cuma gaji. Jika seseorang mau ambil $2.000 untuk pergi, mereka tidak punya komitmen yang dibutuhkan Zappos. Kurang dari 1% yang mengambil tawaran ini.

Hiring for Luck & Kindness

Merekrut Berdasarkan Keberuntungan & Kebaikan

  • The Shuttle Driver Test: Zappos interviews often involve a shuttle ride from the airport. Recruiters check with the driver later: "How did the candidate treat you?" If they were rude to the driver, they are rejected, no matter how smart they are. Tes Supir Jemputan: Wawancara Zappos sering melibatkan jemputan dari bandara. Perekrut cek ke supir nanti: "Gimana kandidat memperlakukanmu?" Jika mereka kasar ke supir, mereka ditolak, tak peduli sepintar apa pun.
  • The "Lucky" Question: Candidates are asked: "On a scale of 1-10, how lucky are you?" Zappos hires people who feel lucky, because they tend to see opportunities rather than problems. Pertanyaan "Beruntung": Kandidat ditanya: "Skala 1-10, seberapa beruntung Anda?" Zappos merekrut orang yang merasa beruntung, karena mereka cenderung melihat peluang daripada masalah.

The Telephone is NOT Dead

Telepon TIDAK Mati

While most companies hide their phone number, Zappos puts it at the top of every page. They view every call as an opportunity to build a lifelong emotional connection.

Sementara kebanyakan perusahaan menyembunyikan nomor telepon, Zappos menaruhnya di bagian paling atas setiap halaman. Mereka melihat setiap panggilan sebagai peluang membangun koneksi emosional seumur hidup.

  • Goal: Get off the phone ASAP.
Zappos Call Center Call Center Zappos
  • NO scripts.
  • NO time limits.
  • Goal: Deliver WOW through service.

Legendary Case Study: The Pizza

Studi Kasus Legendaris: Pizza

Tony was at a conference in Santa Monica. It was late (post-midnight), and room service was closed. His clients were hungry for pepperoni pizza.

Tony bet them: "Call Zappos customer service. I bet they will help you find a pizza."

The client thought he was crazy (Zappos sells shoes!). They put it on speaker. The Zappos rep was confused for 2 seconds, then said: "Hold on." She spent 2 minutes searching and returned with a list of the 3 nearest pizza places open at 2 AM.

The Lesson: Zappos isn't about shoes. It's about solving problems and being helpful.

Tony ada di konferensi di Santa Monica. Sudah larut malam, layanan kamar tutup. Kliennya lapar ingin pizza pepperoni.

Tony bertaruh: "Telepon CS Zappos. Aku taruhan mereka akan bantu kamu cari pizza."

Klien pikir dia gila (Zappos jual sepatu!). Mereka loudspeaker. Rep Zappos bingung 2 detik, lalu bilang: "Tunggu sebentar." Dia mencari 2 menit dan kembali dengan daftar 3 tempat pizza terdekat yang buka jam 2 pagi.

Pelajaran: Zappos bukan tentang sepatu. Ini tentang memecahkan masalah dan membantu.

Record Breaking Call Rekor Panggilan Terlama

The longest call at Zappos lasted 10 hours and 43 minutes. The rep didn't just sell shoes; she chatted, listened, and connected. Zappos celebrated this, they didn't punish it.

Panggilan terlama di Zappos berlangsung 10 jam 43 menit. Rep itu tidak cuma jual sepatu; dia ngobrol, mendengarkan, dan terhubung. Zappos merayakan ini, tidak menghukumnya.

Part 4

The 10 Core Values 10 Nilai Inti

These aren't just corporate jargon. Zappos hires, fires, and reviews performance based on these 10 values.

Ini bukan sekadar jargon perusahaan. Zappos merekrut, memecat, dan meninjau kinerja berdasarkan 10 nilai ini.

1. Deliver WOW Through Service Go above and beyond what is expected. Lakukan lebih dari yang diharapkan.
2. Embrace and Drive Change Don't fear change; initiate it. Jangan takut perubahan; mulailah.
3. Create Fun and A Little Weirdness Be your authentic self, even if it's quirky. Jadilah diri sendiri, meski agak aneh.
4. Be Adventurous, Creative, and Open-Minded Take risks. Mistakes are okay. Ambil risiko. Kesalahan itu oke.
5. Pursue Growth and Learning Zappos has a library. Read and grow. Zappos punya perpustakaan. Baca & tumbuh.
6. Build Open and Honest Relationships Communication via trust. Komunikasi melalui kepercayaan.
7. Build a Positive Team and Family Spirit Coworkers are more than just coworkers. Rekan kerja lebih dari sekadar rekan.
8. Do More With Less Efficiency and innovation. Efisiensi dan inovasi.
9. Be Passionate and Determined Passion is the fuel. Gairah adalah bahan bakarnya.
10. Be Humble No arrogance, no matter how successful. Tidak sombong, sesukses apa pun.
Part 5

The Science of Happiness Sains Kebahagiaan

The 3 Types of Happiness

3 Jenis Kebahagiaan

  • 1. Pleasure (Rock Star) 1. Kesenangan (Bintang Rock) Chasing the next high. Very short-term. Once the stimulus is gone, happiness is gone. (Least sustainable). Mengejar kesenangan sesaat. Sangat jangka pendek. Begitu stimulus hilang, bahagia hilang. (Paling tidak berkelanjutan).
  • 2. Passion (Flow) 2. Gairah (Aliran/Flow) Engagement. Being so absorbed in an activity that time flies. More sustainable than pleasure. Keterlibatan. Begitu larut dalam aktivitas hingga waktu berlalu cepat. Lebih berkelanjutan dari kesenangan.
  • 3. Higher Purpose (Meaning) 3. Tujuan Lebih Tinggi (Makna) Being part of something bigger than yourself. This is the most sustainable form of happiness. Menjadi bagian dari sesuatu yang lebih besar dari diri sendiri. Ini adalah bentuk kebahagiaan paling berkelanjutan.

"Zappos decided to focus on Passion and Higher Purpose. Delivering Happiness to the world is the purpose; selling shoes is just the mechanism."

"Zappos memutuskan untuk fokus pada Gairah dan Tujuan Lebih Tinggi. Mengantarkan Kebahagiaan ke dunia adalah tujuannya; menjual sepatu hanyalah mekanismenya."
