The Philosophy Filosofi

Why People Buy Mengapa Orang Membeli

"People don't like to be sold, but they love to buy." "Orang tidak suka dijual (dipaksa beli), tapi mereka suka membeli."

Jeffrey Gitomer strips away the complexity of sales. He argues that most salespeople fail because they focus on *their* product, *their* quota, and *their* commission. But the customer only cares about one thing: Themselves.

If you try to "sell," you create resistance. If you help them "buy" (solve a problem, feel good, gain value), you create a customer for life. This book is about becoming a resource, not a nuisance.

Jeffrey Gitomer menelanjangi kompleksitas penjualan. Dia berargumen bahwa kebanyakan penjual gagal karena mereka fokus pada produk *mereka*, kuota *mereka*, dan komisi *mereka*. Tapi pelanggan hanya peduli satu hal: Diri Mereka Sendiri.

Jika Anda mencoba "menjual", Anda menciptakan penolakan. Jika Anda membantu mereka "membeli" (memecahkan masalah, merasa senang, mendapatkan nilai), Anda menciptakan pelanggan seumur hidup. Buku ini tentang menjadi sumber daya, bukan pengganggu.

The "Why" vs. The "How"

"Mengapa" vs. "Bagaimana"

Sales training usually teaches you HOW to sell (closing techniques, overcoming objections).

Gitomer teaches you WHY they buy. If you understand the "Why" (they like you, they trust you, it solves a pain), the "How" becomes easy. The "Why" is the DNA of success.

Pelatihan penjualan biasanya mengajarkan Anda BAGAIMANA cara menjual (teknik closing, mengatasi keberatan).

Gitomer mengajarkan Anda MENGAPA mereka membeli. Jika Anda mengerti "Mengapa"-nya (mereka suka Anda, percaya Anda, itu solusi masalah), "Bagaimana"-nya jadi mudah. "Mengapa" adalah DNA kesuksesan.

Part 1

Preparation & Attitude Persiapan & Sikap

1. Kick Your Own Ass

1. Pecut Diri Sendiri (Kick Your Own Ass)

Nobody will do it for you. If you are having a bad sales month, look in the mirror. You are the problem, and you are the solution.

Action: Stop blaming the economy, the marketing department, or the price. Take 100% responsibility. Work while others sleep. Read while others watch TV.

Tidak ada yang akan melakukannya untuk Anda. Jika bulan penjualan Anda buruk, lihat cermin. Anda masalahnya, dan Anda solusinya.

Aksi: Berhenti menyalahkan ekonomi, departemen pemasaran, atau harga. Ambil tanggung jawab 100%. Bekerja saat orang lain tidur. Membaca saat orang lain nonton TV.

3. Personal Branding

3. Personal Branding

"It's not who you know, it's who knows YOU."

If people can't find you on Google, you don't exist. Build a reputation as an expert, not a salesperson. Write articles, give speeches, be visible. Make people call YOU.

"Bukan siapa yang Anda kenal, tapi siapa yang kenal ANDA."

Jika orang tidak bisa menemukan Anda di Google, Anda tidak ada. Bangun reputasi sebagai ahli, bukan penjual. Tulis artikel, beri pidato, jadilah terlihat. Buat orang menelepon ANDA.

Part 2

Value & Networking Nilai & Networking

4. It's All About Value, Not Price

4. Semua Tentang Nilai, Bukan Harga

If you lose a sale on price, itâ€™s not because your price is too high. Itâ€™s because your value was too low.

Jika Anda kehilangan penjualan karena harga, itu bukan karena harga Anda terlalu tinggi. Itu karena nilai Anda terlalu rendah.

Customer says: "Your price is too high."
Gitomer says: "Are you looking for the lowest price or the best cost over time? Cheap products break. My product creates profit." "Anda cari harga terendah atau biaya terbaik jangka panjang? Produk murah gampang rusak. Produk saya hasilkan profit."

5. Networking is Mandatory

5. Networking itu Wajib

Face-to-face selling beats everything else. You must be where your customers are.

Penjualan tatap muka mengalahkan segalanya. Anda harus berada di tempat pelanggan Anda berada.

Case Study: The Baseball Game Studi Kasus: Pertandingan Bisbol

Don't network at "Networking Events" (full of salespeople trying to sell). Network at a baseball game, a charity event, or a country club.
Tactics: Don't pitch. Ask questions. "What do you do?" "How's business?" "Who is your ideal customer?" Be interested, not interesting.

Jangan networking di "Acara Networking" (isinya penjual semua). Networking-lah di pertandingan bisbol, acara amal, atau country club.
Taktik: Jangan pitching. Bertanyalah. "Kerja apa?" "Gimana bisnis?" "Siapa pelanggan ideal Anda?" Jadilah tertarik, bukan menarik.

Humor is the highest form of language mastery. It relaxes the prospect and builds rapport instantly.

Rule: Don't tell jokes (too risky). Use stories. Make fun of yourself, never the customer. If the customer laughs, the barrier goes down.

Humor adalah bentuk tertinggi penguasaan bahasa. Ini menenangkan prospek dan membangun hubungan seketika.

Aturan: Jangan cerita lelucon (terlalu berisiko). Gunakan cerita. Tertawakan diri sendiri, jangan pernah pelanggan. Jika pelanggan tertawa, tembok pertahanan turun.

9. Use Creativity to Differentiate

9. Gunakan Kreativitas untuk Membedakan Diri

If you sound like every other salesperson ("Just checking in..."), you will be treated like every other salesperson (ignored).

Jika Anda terdengar seperti penjual lain ("Cuma mau ngecek..."), Anda akan diperlakukan seperti penjual lain (diabaikan).

Case Study: The Voicemail Studi Kasus: Pesan Suara

Boring: "Hi, this is Jeff calling to see if you got my proposal." (Delete).
Creative: "Hi Dave. I figured out a way to save you $5,000 on that project we discussed. Call me by 5 PM if you want to know how." (Call back guaranteed).

Membosankan: "Halo, ini Jeff mau tanya apa proposal saya sudah sampai." (Hapus).
Kreatif: "Halo Dave. Saya nemu cara buat hemat $5.000 di proyek yang kita bahas. Telepon saya sebelum jam 5 sore kalau mau tahu caranya." (Dijamin telepon balik).

Part 4

Closing & Reducing Risk Closing & Mengurangi Risiko

6. The Real Decision Maker

6. Pengambil Keputusan Asli

If you are pitching to someone who has to say "I need to ask my boss," you are pitching to the wrong person. You are wasting your time.

Tactic: Don't ask "Are you the decision maker?" (They will lie and say yes due to ego).
Ask: "How is the decision made?" or "Who else needs to weigh in on this?" Then, insist on being present when the real boss sees the proposal.

Jika Anda pitching ke orang yang harus bilang "Saya tanya bos dulu," Anda pitching ke orang yang salah. Buang waktu.

Taktik: Jangan tanya "Anda pengambil keputusan?" (Mereka akan bohong bilang ya karena ego).
Tanya: "Bagaimana keputusan dibuat?" atau "Siapa lagi yang perlu pertimbangkan ini?" Lalu, bersikeraslah untuk hadir saat bos asli melihat proposalnya.

10. Reduce Their Risk

10. Kurangi Risiko Mereka

Customers don't buy because they are afraid. Afraid of making a mistake, afraid of losing money, afraid of looking stupid to their boss.

Pelanggan tidak beli karena mereka takut. Takut salah, takut rugi, takut terlihat bodoh di depan bos.

  • Identify the Risk: Ask "What is your biggest concern?"
  • Eliminate it: Offer a guarantee, a trial period, or show overwhelming testimonials. Replace risk with comfort.
Part 5

The Gold Mine (Referrals) Tambang Emas (Referral)

A referral is 100 times more valuable than a cold call. Why? Because the trust is transferred.

Satu referral (rujukan) 100 kali lebih berharga daripada cold call. Kenapa? Karena kepercayaan ditransfer.

Why Salespeople Don't Get Referrals Kenapa Penjual Gak Dapat Referral
  1. They forget to ask.
  2. They ask too late (after the relationship is cold).
  3. They don't deserve them (Service was average, not great).

Gitomer's Law: "Provide service so memorable that they talk about you." When you do that, referrals happen naturally. But you still have to ask: "Who do you know that could benefit from this just like you did?"

Hukum Gitomer: "Berikan layanan yang begitu berkesan hingga mereka membicarakan Anda." Saat Anda lakukan itu, referral terjadi alami. Tapi Anda tetap harus tanya: "Siapa kenalan Anda yang bisa dapat manfaat dari ini seperti Anda?"

Principle 12.5: Resign Your Position as General Manager of the Universe

Prinsip 12.5: Mengundurkan Diri sebagai Manajer Umum Alam Semesta

Stop worrying about things you cannot control (Politics, Weather, Competitors). Focus 100% of your energy on what you CAN control: Your attitude, your preparation, and your actions.

"If you make a sale, you can earn a commission. If you make a friend, you can earn a fortune."

Berhenti mengkhawatirkan hal yang tak bisa Anda kontrol (Politik, Cuaca, Pesaing). Fokuskan 100% energi Anda pada apa yang BISA Anda kontrol: Sikap, persiapan, dan tindakan Anda.

"Jika Anda membuat penjualan, Anda dapat komisi. Jika Anda mencari teman, Anda dapat kekayaan."
