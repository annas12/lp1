The One Minute Manager

Increase Productivity, Profits, and Prosperity Meningkatkan Produktivitas, Keuntungan, dan Kemakmuran

The Concept Konsep Dasar

Autocratic vs. Democratic Otokratis vs. Demokratis

"People who feel good about themselves produce good results." "Orang yang merasa baik tentang diri mereka sendiri menghasilkan hasil yang baik."

The story begins with a young man searching for an effective manager. He finds two types:
1. "Tough" Managers: Focused on results, but their people are unhappy. (Short-term win, long-term loss).
2. "Nice" Managers: Focused on people, but the results are poor. (Democratic but inefficient).

Then he meets the One Minute Manager. He realizes that you don't have to choose between people and results. They are connected.

Cerita dimulai dengan seorang pemuda yang mencari manajer efektif. Dia menemukan dua tipe:
1. Manajer "Keras": Fokus pada hasil, tapi orang-orangnya tidak bahagia. (Menang jangka pendek, rugi jangka panjang).
2. Manajer "Baik": Fokus pada orang, tapi hasilnya buruk. (Demokratis tapi tidak efisien).

Lalu dia bertemu Manajer Satu Menit. Dia sadar bahwa Anda tidak perlu memilih antara orang dan hasil. Keduanya saling terhubung.

The 80/20 Rule of Management

Aturan Manajemen 80/20

80% of your really important results will come from 20% of your goals. The One Minute Manager focuses only on that 20%. This is why they have so much free time.

80% dari hasil Anda yang sangat penting akan datang dari 20% tujuan Anda. Manajer Satu Menit hanya fokus pada 20% itu. Inilah mengapa mereka punya banyak waktu luang.

Secret #1

One Minute Goals Tujuan Satu Menit

The Problem: Silent Expectations

Masalahnya: Harapan Bisu

In most companies, employees don't know what is expected of them. They only find out they did something wrong during the annual performance review. This is like bowling with a curtain in front of the pins. You roll the ball, and the boss says "You missed," but you don't know why.

Di kebanyakan perusahaan, karyawan tidak tahu apa yang diharapkan dari mereka. Mereka baru tahu kalau salah saat evaluasi kinerja tahunan. Ini seperti main bowling dengan tirai di depan pin. Anda lempar bola, dan bos bilang "Meleset," tapi Anda tidak tahu kenapa.

How to Set One Minute Goals Cara Menetapkan Tujuan Satu Menit
  1. Agree on the Goal: Manager and employee agree on what needs to be done. Sepakati Tujuan: Manajer dan karyawan sepakat tentang apa yang harus dilakukan.
  2. Write it Down: Describe the goal in less than 250 words. Tuliskan: Deskripsikan tujuan dalam kurang dari 250 kata.
  3. The 1-Minute Test: You must be able to read it in under one minute. Tes 1 Menit: Anda harus bisa membacanya dalam waktu kurang dari satu menit.
  4. Review Frequently: The employee looks at the goal daily to ensure their behavior matches the goal. Tinjau Sering-Sering: Karyawan melihat tujuan setiap hari untuk memastikan perilaku mereka sesuai tujuan.
Why it works: Mengapa ini berhasil:

It removes the "guesswork." When people know exactly what the target is, they manage themselves. The manager doesn't need to hover.

Ini menghilangkan "tebak-tebakan." Saat orang tahu persis targetnya, mereka mengelola diri sendiri. Manajer tidak perlu mengawasi terus-menerus.

Secret #2

One Minute Praisings Pujian Satu Menit

Most managers practice "Leave Alone / Zap." They say nothing when things are good (Leave Alone), and punish when things go wrong (Zap). The One Minute Manager does the opposite: Catch them doing something right.

Kebanyakan manajer mempraktikkan "Diam / Sentrum." Mereka diam saat semua baik, dan menghukum saat ada yang salah (Sentrum). Manajer Satu Menit melakukan sebaliknya: Tangkap basah mereka melakukan hal yang benar.

Case Study: Training Shamu

Studi Kasus: Melatih Shamu

How do you train a 5-ton killer whale to jump over a rope? You can't punish it (it will eat you).

Step 1: Put the rope at the bottom of the pool.
Step 2: When the whale swims over it, feed it (Positive Reinforcement).
Step 3: Raise the rope 1 inch. Repeat.

Lesson for Humans: In the beginning, you don't wait for "perfect" performance. You praise "Approximately Right" behavior. You help them build confidence until they can clear the high jump.

Bagaimana melatih paus pembunuh 5 ton melompati tali? Anda tidak bisa menghukumnya (dia akan memakan Anda).

Langkah 1: Taruh tali di dasar kolam.
Langkah 2: Saat paus berenang di atasnya, beri makan (Penguatan Positif).
Langkah 3: Naikkan tali 1 inci. Ulangi.

Pelajaran untuk Manusia: Di awal, jangan tunggu kinerja "sempurna". Pujilah perilaku yang "Kira-Kira Benar." Anda bantu mereka bangun percaya diri sampai mereka bisa melompati tali tinggi.

The Praising Formula Rumus Pujian
  • Immediate: Don't save it for a review. Do it now.
  • Specific: Don't say "Good job." Say "I liked how you handled that angry client by listening first." (Proves you were watching).
  • Share Feelings: Tell them how good it makes you feel (helps the company).
  • Pause: Silence for a few seconds to let them feel the praise.
  • Encourage: Shake hands, smile.
Secret #3

One Minute Reprimands (Re-Directs) Teguran Satu Menit (Re-Directs)

If goals are clear and the employee knows how to do the job but makes a mistake, a Reprimand is necessary. Note: If they don't know how, they need Training, not a Reprimand.

Jika tujuan sudah jelas dan karyawan tahu cara melakukan pekerjaan tapi membuat kesalahan, Teguran diperlukan. Catatan: Jika mereka tidak tahu caranya, mereka butuh Pelatihan, bukan Teguran.

The Reprimand Formula (Tough n' Nice)

Rumus Teguran (Tegas & Baik)

Part 1: The Behavior (Tough)
  • Immediate: Do it as soon as the mistake happens.
  • Specific: "You were late to the meeting." (Fact). Not "You are lazy." (Opinion/Attack).
  • Share Feelings: "I am frustrated because we need your input."
  • Pause: Uncomfortable silence. Let it sink in.
Part 2: The Person (Nice)
  • Re-affirm Worth: "You are one of my best people. This isn't like you."
  • Separate: Attack the behavior, not the person. "You are good, but this behavior is unacceptable."
  • It's Over: Once the reprimand is done, it is over. Never bring it up again. No grudges.
Why the "Sandwich" Method Fails Mengapa Metode "Sandwich" Gagal

Traditional managers use the Sandwich: Praise -> Criticize -> Praise.
The One Minute Manager does NOT do this. Why? Because the employee waits for the "but..." and ignores the praise.

The One Minute Way: Be stern first (Correction). Then be supportive (Affirmation). It leaves the person thinking about their mistake but feeling valued as a person.

Manajer tradisional pakai Sandwich: Puji -> Kritik -> Puji.
Manajer Satu Menit TIDAK melakukan ini. Kenapa? Karena karyawan menunggu kata "tapi..." dan mengabaikan pujiannya.

Cara Satu Menit: Tegas dulu (Koreksi). Lalu mendukung (Afirmasi). Ini membuat orang memikirkan kesalahannya tapi tetap merasa dihargai sebagai pribadi.

Part 4

Why it Works (Psychology) Mengapa Ini Berhasil (Psikologi)

1. Feedback Loops 1. Putaran Umpan Balik

Imagine playing video games with your eyes closed. It's boring. You need instant feedback (score) to stay engaged. One Minute Goals gives the target. Praisings/Reprimands provide the score.

Bayangkan main video game dengan mata tertutup. Membosankan. Anda butuh umpan balik instan (skor) agar tetap terlibat. Tujuan Satu Menit memberi target. Pujian/Teguran memberikan skornya.

2. Ego Defense 2. Pertahanan Ego

When you attack a person ("You are stupid"), their ego puts up a wall. They stop listening. When you attack the behavior but affirm the person ("You are smart, but this report is sloppy"), the ego stays down, and they can hear the feedback.

Saat Anda menyerang orang ("Kamu bodoh"), ego mereka membangun tembok. Mereka berhenti mendengar. Saat Anda menyerang perilaku tapi menegaskan orangnya ("Kamu pintar, tapi laporan ini ceroboh"), ego tetap turun, dan mereka bisa mendengar masukannya.

"Goals begin behaviors. Consequences maintain behaviors."

"Tujuan memulai perilaku. Konsekuensi mempertahankan perilaku."
